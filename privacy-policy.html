4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 How Your Data is Stored

All your task data, categories, and preferences are stored locally on your device using the following methods:

  • SharedPreferences: For storing user settings, preferences, and serialized task data
  • Device Storage: All data remains on your device and is never synchronized to cloud services
  • No Cloud Backup: Unless you manually export and backup your data, no copies are maintained on external servers

4.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Encryption: Device-level encryption provided by your operating system
  • Access Control: No data transmission means no network interception risk
  • Secure Storage: We use platform-standard secure storage mechanisms
  • No Network Communication: Your data is never transmitted over the internet (offline-first architecture)
  • Regular Security Updates: We maintain our codebase with the latest security patches

4.3 Data Deletion

You have full control over your data and can:

  • Delete individual tasks at any time
  • Clear all completed tasks with a single action
  • Delete all data from the app using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings
  • Uninstall the app to permanently remove all associated data from your device

4.4 Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the app. When you delete the app or perform a factory reset on your device, all associated data is permanently removed. We do not maintain copies of deleted data.
